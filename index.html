I am a Ph.D student in the School of Electrical and Computer Engineering at Purdue University, advised by Prof. Sabre Kais and Prof. Zubin Jacob. My previous research experiences include microwave synthesis, semi-classical modeling, quantum transport modeling and fabrication of nanoelectronic devices. The interplay between quantum computing and artificial intelligence excites me and in that regard my current research interest lies in quantum machine learning. With regards to quantum information theory, my interests include algorithms and complexity theory, and also in understanding the fundamental limits of information processing.
